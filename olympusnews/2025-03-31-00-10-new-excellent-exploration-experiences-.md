Crossroad Contest is a 10 map contest centered around "macrorouting" where you explore and piece together puzzle pieces in a large map in order to solve steps of progression (not too different from a metroidvania). Maps range from Beginner and Trivial to Low Grandmaster and Confusing. Every single map offers a unique experience, so it's definitely worth checking out all of them!
